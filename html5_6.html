New CSS3 Selectors

The following are common CSS3 selectors illustrated with a basic example:

  • div > p the greater than sign (">") affects the paragraphs that are children of a div element. Only works with a direct parent, NOT a grandparent.
  • p.bucky + p the plus sign ("+") only affects the paragraphs that immediately follow a paragraph of class "bucky." They both MUST share the same parent.
  • p.bucky ~ p the tilde ("~") affects the paragraphs that follow a paragraph of class "bucky," but does NOT need to immediately follow the "bucky" paragraph.
